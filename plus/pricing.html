FAQ

What happens after I subscribe?

PayPal confirms the first payment, we spin up your account on our end, and email you the username, password, and DNS endpoints. You can be watching within a couple of minutes.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, from your dashboard. The service runs to the end of the period you paid for. No refunds, no questions.

How many devices can connect?

One at a time, per account. If you want concurrent streams, sign up a second account.

Do I need to use PayPal?

For v1, yes — PayPal handles the billing and the auto-renewal. We can add more options later.
