Setup guide

Pick your device. All apps use the same Xtream Codes login you got in your welcome email.

We highly recommend TiviMate or Sparkle

Both apps support Xtream Codes login and work great on Android phones, tablets, Android TV, and Fire TV. Install instructions are in the Android and Fire Stick sections below.

Note: any additional payment required by these apps is from the developers of them, not Tree Frog Plus. We do not sell or profit from TiviMate or Sparkle.

📱

Android (phone, tablet, Android TV)

Install TiviMate (recommended) or Sparkle from the official links below, then sign in with your Xtream Codes credentials.

TiviMate

  • Open the Downloader app and enter the code 272483 to fetch the official APK from the developer.
  • Or sideload the APK directly: https://files.tivimate.com/tivimate.apk

Sparkle

  • Install from APKPure: https://d.apkpure.com/b/APK/se.hedekonsult.sparkle?version=latest

Sign in with Xtream Codes

  1. Open the app and choose Login with Xtream Codes API.
  2. Enter the credentials from your welcome email:
    • Server: a DNS endpoint from the box below (apex first).
    • Username: from your welcome email.
    • Password: from your welcome email.
  3. Tap Add User → Login. The app loads the channel list, EPG, and VOD.

Fire Stick / Fire TV

Same apps as Android. We recommend TiviMate for Fire TV.

Get the apps

  1. From the Fire TV home screen, search for and install Downloader (orange icon).
  2. Open Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and turn on Apps from Unknown Sources for Downloader.
  3. In Downloader, enter the code 272483 to install TiviMate from the developer's site. Or enter 4527163 for our unlocked APK (see below).

Tree Frog Plus unlocked APK

We host an APK that's pre-configured for Tree Frog Plus and unlocked for our subscribers at no extra cost. You still sign in with your own credentials.

  • Downloader code: 4527163
  • Direct link: https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=1_3OSnh_wIyxz9FA4Am8gMe2ocwLhsoDi

Sign in

  1. Open the app.
  2. Choose Login with Xtream Codes API.
  3. Enter the same credentials as the Android section above.
Heads up: "Apps from Unknown Sources" stays on for Downloader. You can turn it off again once your app is installed.
📺

Roku

  1. From the Roku home screen, open Streaming Store and search for IPTV Smarters.
  2. Add the channel and open it.
  3. Choose Login with Xtream Codes API and enter the same credentials as Android.
  4. Tap Add User. The channel list will load — EPG shows up automatically.
Tip: if IPTV Smarters is not available in your region's store, the Roku Web Browser can reach your dashboard for the in-browser player.
