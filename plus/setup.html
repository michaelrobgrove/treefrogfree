Setup guide

Pick your device. All apps below use the same Xtream Codes login you got in your welcome email.

📱 Android 🔥 Fire Stick 📺 Roku
📱

Android (phone, tablet, Android TV)

  1. Install IPTV Smarters Pro from the Google Play Store, or sideload TiviMate from the developer's site.
  2. Open the app and choose Login with Xtream Codes API.
  3. Enter the credentials from your welcome email:
    • Server: one of the DNS endpoints below (apex first).
    • Username: from your welcome email.
    • Password: from your welcome email.
  4. Tap Add User → Login. The app will load the channel list, EPG, and VOD.
DNS endpoints (pick one)
https://apex.tfplus.stream
http://comet.tfplus.stream
🔥

Fire Stick / Fire TV

  1. From the Fire TV home screen, search for and install Downloader (orange icon).
  2. Open Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and turn on Apps from Unknown Sources for Downloader.
  3. Open Downloader, enter the code 641178, and install IPTV Smarters.
  4. Open IPTV Smarters, choose Login with Xtream Codes API, and enter the same credentials as Android above.
Heads up: "Apps from Unknown Sources" stays on for that one app. You can turn it off again after IPTV Smarters installs.
📺

Roku

  1. From the Roku home screen, open Streaming Store and search for IPTV Smarters.
  2. Add the channel and open it.
  3. Choose Login with Xtream Codes API and enter:
    • Server: a DNS endpoint from the Android section above.
    • Username & Password: from your welcome email.
  4. Tap Add User. The channel list will load — EPG shows up automatically.
Tip: if IPTV Smarters is not available in your region's store, the Roku Web Browser can reach your dashboard for the in-browser player.
